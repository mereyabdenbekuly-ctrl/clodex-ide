# Contributors and upstream credits

This file separates people who maintain or contribute to Clodex from people
whose work entered the repository through its Stagewise upstream. Upstream
credit does not imply that a person endorses, maintains, or is affiliated with
Clodex.

## Current Clodex maintainer

- [Merey Abdenbekuly](https://github.com/mereyabdenbekuly-ctrl) — project
  founder and bootstrap Core Maintainer

Maintainer roles and their scope are defined in [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md).

## Clodex contributors

People are added to this section after a merged Clodex contribution or another
documented project contribution, with their consent. Git commit authorship and
release notes remain the primary record of individual code changes.

To contribute, read [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md). Regular contributors
may follow the role path described in [`COLLABORATE.md`](COLLABORATE.md).
